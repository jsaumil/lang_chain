Engineering Programs 
Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) 
Program Overview: 
● Duration: 4 Years 
● Eligibility: 10+2 PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Maths) or Equivalent from a Recognised Board + GUJCET (Gujarat Common Entrance Test)
● Admission Process: Admissions at Gandhinagar University is done through The Admission Committee for Professional Courses (ACPC) 
● Tuition Fees: Rs. 31,000/- per semester (Fee Regulatory Committee (FRC) approved) 
Available Specializations and Intake: 
● Civil Engineering: 90 seats 
● Computer Engineering: 300 seats (180+120) 
● Information Technology: 180 seats (120+60) 
● Mechanical Engineering: 180 seats 
● Electrical Engineering: 60 seats 
● Electronics & Communication: 30 seats 
● Computer Science & Engineering: 60 seats 
● Information & Communication Technology: 60 seats 
● Cyber Security: 60 seats 
● Artificial Intelligence: 30 seats

Business Administration Programs 
Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) 
Program Overview: 
● Duration: 3 Years 
● Eligibility: The candidate must have passed 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board in India with English and Accountancy subjects 
● Special Note: Students who have not opted Accountancy as a subject in standard 12 and taken admission into BBA Course, must submit assignment/complete bridge course of Accountancy subject before completion of First Semester by paying ₹ 2000 as additional fees at Gandhinagar University 
● Admission Process: Admissions at Gandhinagar University is done through The Admission Committee of Gandhinagar University, based on Merit 
● Tuition Fees: Rs. 35,000/- per semester 
● Total Sanctioned Intake: 700 seats 
Specializations Offered in Final Year: 
● Accounting and Auditing 
● Banking, Financial Services and Insurance 
● Statistics

Important Notes for Applicants 
Admission Committees: 
● ACPC (Admission Committee for Professional Courses): Handles admissions for Engineering, Medical, MBA, and some other professional courses 
● Gandhinagar University Admission Committee: Handles admissions for most undergraduate and postgraduate programs based on merit 
Fee Structure: 
● All fees mentioned are per semester unless otherwise specified 
● Additional fees may apply for certain programs (e.g., BBA Accountancy bridge course) 
● Fees are subject to change as per university policy 
General Eligibility Notes: 
* All programs require completion from a recognized board/university Specific percentage requirements may vary by program 
* Age limits apply to certain programs, particularly nursing courses 
* Some programs have specific subject requirements (PCM(Physics, Chemistry, Maths) / PCB(Physics, Chemistry, Biology) etc.) 
Application Process: 
● Merit-based admissions for most programs 
● Entrance exam requirements for specific programs 
● Documentation required as per university guidelines 
● Specific application deadlines as announced by the university
This document contains the complete admission information for Gandhinagar University programs. For the most current information, please refer to the official university website or contact the admission office directly.
